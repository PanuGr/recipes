Lenten Giouvetsi (Katsarolas)

A delicious vegan pasta bake for fasting periods

What You'll Need

🍝 Main Ingredients

  • 🍝
    500g Kritharaki (orzo pasta)
  • 🍅
    400g Crushed tomatoes
  • 🧅
    2 Onions, diced
  • 🧄
    3 cloves Garlic, minced

🌿 Seasonings

  • 🫒
    100ml Olive oil
  • 🌿
    1 tbsp Dried oregano
  • 🍃
    1 tsp Dried mint
  • 🧂
    to taste Salt & pepper
  • 💧
    1L Vegetable broth
15
Prep Minutes
45
Cook Minutes
6
Servings

Preparation Method

1

Sauté Aromatics

In a deep pan, sauté onions in olive oil until soft. Add garlic and cook until fragrant.

2

Add Base

Stir in crushed tomatoes, oregano, mint, salt, and pepper. Cook for 5 minutes.

3

Combine

Add kritharaki and vegetable broth. Bring to boil then reduce to simmer.

4

Bake

Transfer to oven at 180°C (350°F) for 30 minutes until liquid is absorbed.

Nutritional Breakdown

This vegan dish provides complex carbohydrates from the pasta and lycopene from the tomatoes, with healthy fats from olive oil.

Perfect for fasting periods or vegan diets!
