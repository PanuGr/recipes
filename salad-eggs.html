The 4-Step Method

1

Prep Veggies

Slice the cabbage, grate the carrots, and dice the cucumber. Combine them all in a large bowl.

2

Add Protein

Boil the eggs. Slice the whites and cube the cheese, then add them to the salad bowl. Set the yolks aside.

3

Create Sauce

In a separate bowl, mash the egg yolks and whisk vigorously with all other sauce ingredients until smooth.

4

Combine & Serve

Pour the creamy sauce over the salad and toss gently until everything is perfectly coated. Enjoy immediately!
