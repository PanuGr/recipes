Simple 3-Step Method

1

Prep & Combine

In a large bowl, combine diced cucumbers, chopped red onion, corn, cream cheese, mayonnaise, cubed avocado, fresh dill, and boiled chicken breast.

2

Season & Mix

Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper to the bowl. Cover and shake or mix vigorously until all ingredients are well combined.

3

Serve & Enjoy

Open and enjoy this delicious protein-packed salad that makes a complete, nutritious meal!
