The 5-Step Method

1

Cook Apples

Cut apples into pieces and boil for 10 minutes until soft.

2

Melt Chocolate

Chop chocolate and melt using double boiler method.

3

Blend & Mix

Drain apples, blend until smooth, then mix with melted chocolate.

4

Add Biscuits

Break biscuits into mixture and incorporate thoroughly.

5

Chill & Serve

Press into lined dish, refrigerate 3-4 hours, then slice and garnish.
