Creamy Salmon Pasta

An Easy Meal You'll Love!

What You'll Need

🍝 Main Ingredients

  • 🐟
    400g Salmon fillet

    350g Cherry tomatoes
  • 🧀
    300g Cream cheese
  • 🥬
    60g Spinach

🌿 Seasonings & Extras

  • 🫒

    For serving Graviera cheese

Servings

Simple 4-Step Method

1

Prepare Oven Dish

In a 28-30cm baking dish, layer salmon pieces, cherry tomatoes, spinach, cream cheese, olive oil, salt, pepper, and oregano.

2

Bake

Bake in preheated oven at 200°C (392°F) for 20-25 minutes until vegetables are tender.

3

Cook Pasta

Meanwhile, cook pasta spirals in salted boiling water for 2 minutes less than package instructions.

4

Combine & Serve

Mash baked ingredients with a fork, add drained pasta and Graviera cheese. Mix well and serve immediately!
